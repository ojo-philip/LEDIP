LEDIP is an educational platform wholly dedicated to Language Education and digitalised pedagogical sensibilities. It covers the intricacies of language learning, acquisition, academic research writings, language policy and planning and classroom instructional strategies for the 21st Century Scholars.

Mission

Our mission is to serve as a functional hub for Language Educators, Applied Linguists. Sociolinguists and Second Language Scholars in the bid to enhance the dynamism of Language Education.

Vision

  • Unlocking human linguistic ingenuity, creativity and communicative services
  • Promotion of language learning through digital literacy
  • Assisting learners in academic research and professional development
  • Raising a vibrant language teaching and learning community through interactive connectivity.
